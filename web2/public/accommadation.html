Samphire Rottnest

Discover the barefoot luxury and beachfront allure of Samphire Rottnest, a boutique accommodation nestled in the heart of Thomson Bay. With 80 spacious rooms available, each has been thoughtfully designed to complement the natural surroundings, offering an understated modern luxury. From the comfort of your room, marvel at the island's beauty from your own private balcony or deck.

Step outside and traverse the native landscaping, or unwind in the stylish poolside cabanas. The pool offers heated waters in the warmer months and refreshing cool temperatures over the summer. Our conference provides specifically assigned accommodation for our participants. However, availability is limited, so we recommend booking early. Reserving directly through our site entitles you to exclusive discount offers for specific rooms during the conference days. A delightful breakfast will also be included with your stay.

Other options

Experience a historic stay at the Heritage Cottages, located at the southern end of Vincent Way, North Thomson. These cottages, dating back to the 1840s, offer a unique charm and transport you back to earlier times. For a more relaxing beach break or family vacation, opt for Discovery Holiday Parks Rottnest Island. With a variety of deluxe to standard family tents, these eco-friendly accommodations are located near Garden Lake and the beach.

Karma Rottnest offers a laid-back heritage hotel stay with a range of room types from premium lakeside rooms to understated Palm Court rooms. This budget-friendly option features a pool and an onsite restaurant offering delicious Mediterranean-inspired dishes. Another great alternative is the Rottnest Island Authority Accommodation, managing a range of options from premium view cabins and chalets to budget-friendly bungalows and dorms, including a family-friendly camping ground near The Basin beach.
