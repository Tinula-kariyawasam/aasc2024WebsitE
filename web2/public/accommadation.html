Samphire Rottnest

Discover the barefoot luxury and beachfront allure of Samphire Rottnest, a boutique accommodation nestled in the heart of Thomson Bay. With 80 spacious rooms available, each has been thoughtfully designed to complement the natural surroundings, offering an understated modern luxury. From the comfort of your room, marvel at the island's beauty from your own private balcony or deck.

Step outside and traverse the native landscaping, or unwind in the stylish poolside cabanas. The pool offers heated waters in the warmer months and refreshing cool temperatures over the summer. Our conference provides specifically assigned accommodation for our participants. However, availability is limited, so we recommend booking early. Reserving directly through our site entitles you to exclusive discount offers for specific rooms during the conference days. A delightful breakfast will also be included with your stay.
