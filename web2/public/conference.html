Conference Details

All costs mentioned are in AUD. Your registration can cover a variety of options including the Workshop, the Conference, Single Day attendance, or Accommodation. The Workshop and Single Day attendance includes lunch and morning/afternoon tea. The Conference package includes attendance, all lunches and morning/afternoon teas, a welcome dinner buffet, canapé cocktails, a conference dinner, and return ferry tickets. The Accommodation option includes a 3-night stay with breakfast included. If you purchase a single day ticket, please email tinula31@gmail.com for further information.

Now, we are offering an opportunity for attendees to register for accommodation only to secure a spot at the Sapphire Stay at a discounted rate. For more details, you may visit our Accommodation page. Also, to make a payment for your registration, kindly head over to our Payment page.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances may change. If you cancel your registration before 1 July 2024, you will be eligible for a full refund. However, for cancellations after 1 July 2024 you are not entitled to any refund.  The refunded proportion will depend on the proximity of the cancellation date to the event. We highly encourage you to email us at tinula31@gmail.com for specific details about our cancellation policy, to provide reasons for cancellation, or for any further clarification.
