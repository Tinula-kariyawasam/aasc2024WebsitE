Conference Details

  • All costs are in AUD.
  • Registration options include;
    • Workshop (includes lunch and teas),
    • Conference (attendance, meals, events, and ferry tickets),
    • Single Day attendance (includes lunch and teas),
    • Accommodation (3-night stay with breakfast).
  • If purchasing a single day ticket, email tinula31@gmail.com for more details.
  • Discounted rate available for the Sapphire Stay accommodation. See the Accommodation page.
  • For payment information, visit the Payment page.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances may change. If you cancel your registration before 1 August 2024, you will be eligible for a full refund. However, for cancellations after 1 August 2024 you are not entitled to any refund.  The refunded proportion will depend on the proximity of the cancellation date to the event. We highly encourage you to email us at tinula31@gmail.com for specific details about our cancellation policy, to provide reasons for cancellation, or for any further clarification.
