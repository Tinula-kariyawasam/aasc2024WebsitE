Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances may change, hence we offer a fair cancellation policy. If you cancel your registration more than 3 months prior to the event, you will be eligible for a full refund. However, for cancellations made less than 3 months from the event, only a proportion of the payment will be refunded, unless a valid reason is provided. The refunded proportion will depend on the proximity of the cancellation date to the event. We highly encourage you to email us at tinula31@gmail.com for specific details about our cancellation policy, to provide reasons for cancellation, or for any further clarification.
