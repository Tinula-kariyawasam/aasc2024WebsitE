About Rottnest

  • The ferry for the conference departs from Fremantle to Rottnest Island. The return ferry travel is complementary with conference registration.
  • Travel options from Perth Airport to Fremantle:
    • Public Transport: Airport Line and Fremantle Line
    • Ride Hailing: Uber, Didi, etc.
    • There are no coference buses organised.
  • Direct flights to Rottnest Island are available from Jandakot airport.
  • At this stage we expected flight times are 2pm Monday 2 September (Jandakot to Rottnest) and 3pm Thursday 5 September (Rottnest to Jandakot)..
